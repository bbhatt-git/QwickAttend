# QwickAttend

**The seamless attendance tracking solution for modern educators.**

QwickAttend is a web application designed to simplify and automate the attendance process for teachers. Leveraging modern technology like QR codes and NFC, it makes tracking student attendance fast, accurate, and effortless.

---

## ‚ú® Key Features

- **Effortless Scanning**: Use your device's camera to scan student QR codes or tap NFC tags for instant attendance marking.
- **Manual Attendance**: A robust fallback system to manually mark students as present, absent, or on leave.
- **Real-time Dashboard**: Get an immediate overview of today's attendance statistics with a clean, easy-to-read interface.
- **Comprehensive Records**: View detailed attendance logs by day or search for an individual student's complete history.
- **Holiday Management**: Easily schedule single or multi-day school holidays in advance for the entire academic year.
- **Data Export**: Export monthly or summary attendance reports to formats like Excel for administrative use.
- **Student Management**: Maintain a clear list of all students, with easy access to their information and unique QR codes.
- **Responsive Design**: Fully functional on both desktop and mobile devices, allowing you to take attendance anywhere.
